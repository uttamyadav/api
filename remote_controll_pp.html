Privacy Policy – Remote Controller for PS

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Remote Controller for PS app (referred to as "app") handles your personal information. The app is provided by Video Studio & Offline Cinema (referred to as "service provider"). This policy applies to all users of the app.

Information Collection and Use

Data Collection

Our app does not collect personal data. However, third-party services such as AdMob, Google Analytics, and Firebase may collect certain non-personal information for analytics, advertising, and crash reporting purposes.

These third-party services may collect certain data to function effectively. Below is a breakdown of the data collected, its purpose, and how it is used.

1. Google Analytics for Firebase

Data Collected:

  • Device information (model, OS version, device identifiers)
  • App usage data (session duration, feature interactions, events triggered)
  • Crash reports (in case of app failures)

Purpose of Collection:

  • Monitor app performance and user engagement
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Analyze user behavior to improve features
  • Track app installs and measure marketing campaign effectiveness

Data Usage:

The collected data is aggregated for analytical purposes and does not personally identify users.

2. AdMob (Google Ad Services)

Data Collected:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • App usage data (e.g., interactions with ads)

Purpose of Collection:

  • Display relevant advertisements based on user interests
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Prevent fraudulent activities and ensure compliance with Google’s ad policies

Data Usage:

Personalized ads are based on user behavior and preferences.

3. Firebase Crashlytics

Data Collected:

  • Crash logs and diagnostic data
  • Device model and OS version
  • App version and session data

Purpose of Collection:

  • Identify and diagnose app crashes
  • Improve app stability and fix bugs
  • Ensure a smoother user experience

Data Usage:

The data collected is used strictly for debugging and crash analysis.

For more details, please review the privacy policies of these third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Permissions Required

Internet Permission

Purpose: This permission is used to connect the app to the internet for remote control and streaming features.
Details: It allows the app to establish a connection with your PS5 console and fetch required data for remote access. No personal browsing data is collected.

Access Network State Permission

Purpose: This permission checks your device's current network status to ensure a stable connection for remote play.
Details: It helps determine if your device is connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data and whether the network is strong enough for remote functionality.

Vibrate Permission

Purpose: This permission allows the app to trigger vibration feedback during gameplay for an immersive experience.
Details: It is only used to enhance your interaction with the app and does not affect other device functions.

High Sampling Rate Sensors Permission

Purpose: This permission enables smoother and more accurate motion control during remote gaming sessions.
Details: It helps the app read device sensor data at a higher frequency for better responsiveness. No sensor data is stored or shared.

Log Data

Collection:

In the event of an app error, the app may collect log data through third-party products on your device. This includes information such as device name, operating system version, and app usage details.

Purpose:

Log data is collected for the purpose of troubleshooting and improving app functionality. This data is not used for any other purposes.
