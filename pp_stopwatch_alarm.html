Privacy Policy for Stopwatch & Alarm App

Introduction

Welcome to the Stopwatch & Alarm App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app. Please review it carefully to understand our practices.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or process any personal data. It accesses only the necessary features required to function, such as alarm and stopwatch functionalities.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Storage Permission - To store alarm data and settings locally on your device.
  • Notifications Permission - To send alarm notifications when an alarm is triggered.

No other permissions or access to your device’s data is requested or required by this app.

No Ads, No Third-Party Services

This app does not display any ads and does not integrate with third-party services that collect data. We are fully committed to providing a clean, ad-free, and privacy-focused experience for our users.

Data Security

The app is designed to prioritize user security. Since no personal data is collected, the risk of breaches or unauthorized access is minimized. All alarm settings and data are stored locally on your device.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it as soon as possible.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or regulations. The "Last Updated" date will be revised, and the updated policy will be available within the app or on our website.
